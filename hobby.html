My Hobbies

Swimming

Surfing

Climbing

Welcome to my world of adventure! As a passionate outdoor enthusiast, I find joy and inspiration in three main hobbies: swimming, surfing, and climbing. These activities have been a significant part of my life, allowing me to push my limits, connect with nature, and challenge myself both physically and mentally.

As a competitive swimmer, I have been a proud member of TCNJ's Men's Varsity swim team (DIII NCAA) and Cherry Hill Highschool West's Varsity swim team (MVP 4x in a row). I have also excelled as a member of the Mount Laurel YMCA Phoenix (YMCA National team 2x years) and Erlton Swim Club Tricounty Swimming League (14 summers). Swimming has taught me discipline, perseverance, and teamwork, as I balance my time between rigorous training sessions and academic responsibilities.

Surfing, on the other hand, has been my source of freedom and thrill in the ocean. From riding the waves of my favorite breaks to conquering storms, I have developed a deep connection with the sea. I have also honed my skills in board repairs, ensuring that my surfboards are always in top shape for my adventures. Surfing has taught me to appreciate the power and beauty of nature, and to always strive for progress and improvement in my craft.

Climbing has been my latest passion, allowing me to explore new heights and conquer challenging terrains. From bouldering to rock climbing, I have developed technical skills, mental strength, and a deep appreciation for the mountains. I have climbed various locations, and I have ambitious plans for the future, including climbing mountains and exploring new destinations.

As a dedicated student, I understand the importance of balancing my academic responsibilities with my passions. These hobbies have taught me time management, determination, and resilience, as I strive to excel in both my studies and my outdoor pursuits. With a strong drive for self-improvement, I am constantly pushing myself to achieve new heights in both my academic and personal endeavors.

Join me in my exciting journey of adventure, passion, and self-growth as I continue to pursue my love for swimming, surfing, and climbing. Follow along as I share my experiences, stories, and photos of my outdoor escapades. Thank you for joining me on this exhilarating ride!
