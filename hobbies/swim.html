Swimming

TCNJ's Men's Varsity Swim Team (DIII NCAA)

I currently swim on the TCNJ's Men's Varsity Swim Team. It's a Division III NCAA team that competes in the New Jersey Athletic Conference. It's a great team to be a part of and I'm grateful for the opportunity to swim with them.

Cherry Hill High School West's Varsity Swim Team (MVP 4x in a row)

I swam for Cherry Hill High School West's Varsity Swim Team while I was in high school. I was fortunate enough to be named MVP for four consecutive years. It was a great experience and I learned a lot about swimming and myself during my time on the team.

Mount Laurel YMCA Phoenix (YMCA National Team 2x years)

I swam for the Mount Laurel YMCA Phoenix during my time in middle school and high school. It was a YMCA national team and I was fortunate enough to make the team and compete at nationals twice during my time there. It was a great experience and I made a lot of lifelong friends.

Erlton Swim Club Tricounty Swimming League (14 summers)

I started swimming for Erlton Swim Club when I was four years old and continued to swim there for fourteen summers. It was a great community and I loved being a part of the team. I learned a lot about swimming and teamwork during my time there.
